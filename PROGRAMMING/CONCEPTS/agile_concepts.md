## A Balanced Team Approach to Reliability

* https://labspractices.com/guides/reliability/

* Software reliability - software that is anti-fragile and benefits from strain on its system.
    * Includes concerns about availability, latency, security, and data durability, to regulatory requirements for auditing system interactions, to accessibility concerns, and more.

* From the beginning of a products inception, consider the following:
    * (1) articulate reliability outcomes in terms of user expectations and business needs
    * (2) prioritize outcomes by impact and likelihood so that the team has a good sense for where to start.
